Books and Resources for Sadness.

Sadness goes hand in hand with joy, as both of them speak to what really matters to you. Here are some books, podcasts, and resources that can help you navigate sadness well.

The Rabbit Listened - Cori Doerrfeld

A terrible thing happened! See how each animal responds to Taylor. When bad things happen in your life, how do others around you tend to react? How do you react when bad things happen to your loved ones?

Necessary Endings - Dr. Henry Cloud

“Life is full of hellos and goodbyes.” If we don’t know how to say goodbyes well, we become too bogged down to be able to welcome other new good things in life.

Learn to recognize what’s sucking the life out of you and release the people, experiences, and things you’d held onto for far too long.

The Grief Recovery Handbook - John W. James & Russell Friedman

Grief is a messy, non-linear process.

This is a step-by-step guide to help those whose feet have been pulled out from underneath them regain their footing so they can step forth again with deeper meaning and greater courage.
