Books and Resources for Angry.

Anger, though labeled as a “BAD” emotion, can actually help people PROTECT what matters most and PURSUE what they want in life. Here are some books, podcasts, and resources that can help you wield anger well.

Rage Becomes Her: The Power of Women’s Anger - Soraya Chemaly

Women are often receive messages that they need to be quiet, compliant, happy, soft, and weak to be accepted and considered good in society.

Soraya Chemaly explores the toxic effects that women’s suppression of anger has on their personal and professional lives and relationships and offers other ways of wielding anger well.
