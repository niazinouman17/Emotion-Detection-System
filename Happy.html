Books and Resources for Happy.

We live in a society addicted to happiness, but does not know true joy. Discover the nuts and bolt of joy that is not the absence of the hard parts of life, but one that persists nevertheless.

The Courage to Be Happy - Ichiro Kishimi & Fumitake Koga

There are a good number of us who have an allergic reaction to joy. When good things happen, the immediate thoughts are:

Can I trust this?

When’s the other shoe going to drop?

Good things happen to other people, but not to me.

The Joy of Not Working - Ernie J. Zelinski

In a society where busyness as a badge of honor, it’s countercultural to be still, rest, and be unproductive.

Create a new rhythm that honors you as a human being, not human doing.
